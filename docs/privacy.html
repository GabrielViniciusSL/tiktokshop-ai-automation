Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-11

This Privacy Policy describes how the TikTokShop AI Automation web application ("Service") collects, uses, and protects information.

1. Data We Process

  • OAuth tokens issued by TikTok to perform authorized actions (e.g., upload videos, fetch research insights).
  • Operational logs/metrics needed to run the Service (e.g., job status, timestamps).
  • No selling of personal data.

2. How We Use Data

  • To authenticate you and call TikTok APIs you explicitly authorize.
  • To generate and upload content you request (including drafts).
  • To show aggregated analytics and improve the Service.

3. Storage & Security

  • Access tokens are stored server‑side with restricted access.
  • Data at rest is encrypted as provided by our infrastructure providers (e.g., database, storage).
  • We follow least‑privilege access and rotate secrets when needed.

4. Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties, except processors strictly necessary to operate the Service (e.g., hosting, database) under contract.

5. Retention

We retain data only as long as needed to provide the Service or as required by law. You may request deletion of your tokens/content.

6. Your Choices

  • You can revoke TikTok permissions at any time via TikTok settings.
  • You can request deletion of stored tokens/logs by contacting us.

7. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the age defined by local law).

8. International Transfers

Data may be processed in other countries by our infrastructure providers. We use reasonable safeguards for such transfers.

9. Changes

We may update this Policy. Continued use of the Service after changes means you accept the new Policy.

10. Contact

Questions or requests? Email: youremail@example.com
